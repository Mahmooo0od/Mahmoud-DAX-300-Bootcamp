# 🚀 Mahmoud-DAX-300-Bootcamp

Welcome to my personal DAX mastery journey! This repository is a structured training ground designed to simulate a **"LeetCode for DAX"** experience. Using the industry-standard **Contoso Retail Dataset**, I’ve curated and solved 300 challenges to push the boundaries of analytical engineering.

---

## 🎯 The Mission
The goal of this bootcamp is to bridge the gap between "knowing DAX functions" and "solving complex business logic." By solving 300 problems, I focus on:
* **Evaluation Context:** Mastering Row Context vs. Filter Context.
* **Context Transition:** Expert use of `CALCULATE` and `KEEPFILTERS`.
* **Time Intelligence:** Building custom fiscal and relative time calculations.
* **Performance:** Writing efficient DAX to handle large-scale data.

---

## 📊 Roadmap: The 300 Challenges
The bootcamp is divided into three distinct levels of difficulty:

| Level | Focus Area | Status |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
